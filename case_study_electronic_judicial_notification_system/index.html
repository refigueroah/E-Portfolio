The Challenge: Breaking the Paper Barrier

For decades, legal notifications relied on physical couriers and manual paperwork, leading to months of delays in court proceedings. This project aimed to digitize the entire notification lifecycle, ensuring legal certainty through cryptographically secure digital communications.

Strategic Framework

I led the business analysis to align technical requirements with strict legal regulations, ensuring that digital notifications carried the same weight as physical signatures.
