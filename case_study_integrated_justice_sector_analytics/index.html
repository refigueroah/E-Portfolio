Big Data & Digital Transformation

Integrated Justice Sector Analytics Framework

Volume: 6.3M+ Records Impact: Institutional Transparency Role: Lead IT Strategy

Project Overview

This initiative transformed 11 years of raw criminal case data into an actionable Business Intelligence tool. By synthesizing complex data streams from national prosecutors, we created a centralized reporting framework that serves as the "Single Source of Truth" for the entire justice sector.

The Strategic Approach

As a Senior Analyst, I led the technical effort to:

  • storage Aggregate data from 20+ regional offices into a unified SQL-based data warehouse.
  • query_stats Define statistical models to measure criminal prosecution efficacy.
  • visibility Deploy executive dashboards that visualize real-time crime trends by geography and case type.

Key Results

Evidence-Based Planning

Enabled leadership to allocate resources based on crime hotspots rather than intuition.
